Supported MOD List for Custom UE4SS build for Trials of Mana

This page provides a list of MODs compatible with Custom UE4SS build for Trials of Mana.

日本語 English

Ultra Dash

Type: Movement

A MOD that increases movement speed to 2x-3x. You can switch speed modes in-game. Great for reducing time during repeat playthroughs. In 3x mode, you can avoid many enemy attacks by running, so it's actually useful in battle.

View on NexusMods

Double Jump and Boosted Air Dodge

Type: Movement

Enables double jump and significantly extends air-dodge distance. You'll be able to jump over most enemies with a jump dodge. You can also use double jumps to take shortcuts on some maps. I think this is a mod that's well worth installing.

View on NexusMods

Just Dodge Counter

Type: Combat

Adds meaningful rewards to just dodge, which is underused in the base game. You can unleash powerful counterattacks from slow motion. Recommended for players who want more excitement in battle.

View on NexusMods

Power Up Advanced

Type: Combat

Adds attack-speed increase to the Power Up buff. The high-speed attacks under buff feel highly addictive. It also makes combo attacks easier, helping you break enemy guard gauges more effectively.

View on NexusMods

Simple Photo Mode and Slow Mode

Type: Extras

Adds a simple photo mode to the base game. Recommended if you want to capture attractive character screenshots. It also includes slow mode to make it easier to find the best timing for shots.

View on NexusMods

Costume Randomizer

Type: Miscellaneous

Randomly switches costumes after each battle. Great if you want to enjoy many class outfits without repeatedly changing them in menus. It keeps each encounter feeling fresh.

View on NexusMods

Perfect Debuff

Type: Combat

Improves inconvenient behavior and bugs in debuff skills, including break-type skill and down boost. Recommended for players who want more build variety. It also makes debuff strength easier to read visually, expanding your tactical options in combat.

View on NexusMods

Infinite Drops

Type: Miscellaneous

A MOD that automatically restores items used during battle. This MOD also works in No Future mode. It's recommended for those who don't want to disrupt the game balance but find replenishing items troublesome.

View on NexusMods

Master All Classes for Heroines

Type: Combat

Allows you to transform into any class during battle and use that class's special attacks and magic. Female-characters only. It greatly expands what you can do in combat, and lets you enjoy the thrill of overwhelming your enemies as a heroine who has mastered every class.

View on NexusMods

Request Class Strike

Type: Combat

Enables you to trigger allies' Class Strikes from the ring command during battle. It helps solve common frustrations, such as allies never using their Class Strikes or using them at the worst time right before a boss's major attack. Recommended for players who feel frustrated with AI behavior in combat.

View on NexusMods

Magic Crystal Drops

Type: Combat

The MOD that makes magic attacks drop a small amount of blue crystals. This makes it easier for characters like Angela or Charlotte, who struggle with normal attacks, to build up SP. Recommended when you want to enjoy combat while controlling them, or when you want them in the party as support.

View on NexusMods

Guts of Mana

Type: Combat

This MOD adds a "guts" mechanic to ally characters — when they have enough HP remaining, they survive a killing blow with 1 HP instead of being knocked out. Even on high difficulty where enemies hit harder, your allies will keep fighting instead of dropping instantly. Recommended when you're tired of constantly using a Cup of Wishes.
